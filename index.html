About SafeBite

SafeBite is a revolutionary food safety monitoring system that combines cutting-edge AI technology with practical solutions to ensure food safety in homes, restaurants, and commercial kitchens.

Our mission is to reduce food waste, prevent foodborne illnesses, and provide peace of mind through intelligent monitoring and predictive analytics.
